Linda is a dynamic and experienced nutrition consultant in private practice at Bondi Junction. Specialising in practical advice for healthy living, including weight management, diabetes, gastrointestinal and cardiac conditions as well as nutritional support. With experience gained in hospital and media areas together with unparalleled private practice knowledge she has developed a broad range of dietary services with matching behavioural strategies that will suit you. It is Linda’s unique integrative approach combining the latest scientific, medical, behavioural and psychological components that ensures your success at meeting your goals.

Visit in the comfortable and discrete surroundings of a heritage terrace to discuss your health in professional confidence. Feel free to bring along a support person with you if required.

What you can expect from your first visit? Linda will discuss your goals, take extensive assessment & anthropometry measurements. Linda uses eating guides with behaviour modification strategies to achieve your target.

An initial consultation is best followed up with a review. Ongoing regular review visits are an important part of success. Progress with food recall, anthropometric data and satisfaction will be monitored. You will receive feedback and further adjustments to your behavioural strategies. Attending these review visits in person is best but if you’re are unable to do so then Skype, Phone or Home visit consultations can be arranged.

Private Heath Funds provide different levels of dietetics cover, please check with your fund for details. Chronic Disease Management, (CDM), Enhanced Primary Care Plan, (EPC), and Medicare rebates will attract a gap fee. DVA welcome. With over 30 years of broad-ranging experience, Linda Cumines is one of Australia’s leading Accredited Practising Dietitians and Accredited Nutritionists.
