Diet Rescue

B Sc, Dip Nutrition & Dietetics, Accredited Practicing Dietitian - APD

Linda is a dynamic and experienced nutrition consultant in private practice at Bondi Junction. Specialising in practical advice for healthy living, including weight management, diabetes, gastrointestinal and cardiac conditions as well as nutritional support.

With experience gained through hospital, media and private practice she has developed a broad range of dietary services and behavioural strategies to best suit you. You will benefit from Linda’s unique integrative approach combining the latest scientific information with day to day living.

Whilst attending consultations in person is best, alternative options include: appointments outside of hours, Skype, phone or home visit consultations.

Private Heath Funds provide varying dietetics cover, please check with your fund for details. Chronic Disease Management, (CDM), Enhanced Primary Care Plan, (EPC), and Medicare rebates will attract a gap fee. DVA welcome.

With over 30 years of broad-ranging experience, Linda Cumines is one of Australia’s leading Accredited Practising Dietitians and Accredited Nutritionists.
